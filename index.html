About Me

Your health, your way

My approach considers the bigger picture. Together, we'll build a deeper understanding of your body and how to nourish it in a way that works for you. There is no one-size-fits-all when it comes to nutrition; your plan is tailored to your lifestyle, preferences, and goals.

RED-S · Female Athlete Nutrition
Energy Availability & RED-S

Relative Energy Deficiency in Sport (RED-S) is widely misunderstood and chronically under-diagnosed, even in active women who don't consider themselves athletes. When energy intake doesn't match your output, the consequences ripple across hormones, bone health, immunity, mood, and performance. I help you identify the signs, rebuild adequate fuelling, and restore your body's trust without fear or restriction.

Hormonal health Bone density Performance fuelling Menstrual recovery Active women
🌸
Perimenopause · Menopause · Post-menopause
Menopause Support

The menopause transition is one of the most profound nutritional windows in a woman's life, and one of the least supported. Shifting oestrogen and progesterone affect everything from metabolism and gut health to sleep, mood, and cardiovascular risk. I combine nutritional strategy, targeted herbal remedies, and lifestyle tools to help you navigate this transition with clarity, energy, and confidence rather than just riding it out.

Hormonal balance Metabolism Sleep quality Herbal support Bone health
☀️
Circadian Nutrition · Sustained Vitality
Energy & Circadian Rhythms

Your body has an internal clock, and when you eat matters as much as what you eat. Circadian-aligned nutrition is one of the most exciting emerging fields, with mounting evidence that meal timing, light exposure, and daily rhythm all shape energy, metabolic health, and sleep. I translate this cutting-edge science into simple, practical patterns that keep you energised from morning to evening without relying on caffeine or willpower.

Meal timing Adrenal health Blood sugar stability Cortisol rhythm Fatigue recovery
🦠
Microbiome · Digestive Health
Gut Health

The gut is the cornerstone of almost everything: immunity, mood, hormones, skin, energy, and inflammation all trace back here. Whether you're navigating IBS, bloating, irregular digestion, or simply want to build a more resilient microbiome, I use the latest evidence on diversity, prebiotic and probiotic foods, the gut-brain axis, and targeted herbal support to restore balance from the inside out.

Microbiome diversity IBS & bloating Gut-brain axis Fermented foods Herbal bitters
