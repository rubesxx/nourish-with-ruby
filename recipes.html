The Recipe Collection

Fall in love
with food

Food has always been at the heart of my life. Growing up with an Indian dad, a grandma who made everything from scratch, and a mum completely obsessed with making vegetables taste extraordinary, I was lucky to inherit a love of flavour and a proper set of kitchen skills.

Bringing people together around the table is my craft. Every recipe here is tried and tested, most of them regulars at dinner parties or cooked simply as an act of love. Each one celebrates the functional properties of its ingredients while proving, hopefully convincingly, that vegetables can be genuinely indulgent.

Batch cooking tip: All of the bean sauces freeze beautifully. Double the sauce, portion into freezer bags and you have a quick meal on hand whenever you need it. The crispy toppings (kale, aubergine, seeds) are always best made fresh. The overnight oats keep in the fridge for up to 3 days — ideal for prepping at the start of the week.
